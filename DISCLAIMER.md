# Privacy and Ethics Disclaimer

## IMPORTANT NOTICE

This environmental sound classification system is a **RESEARCH DEMONSTRATION** and is **NOT INTENDED FOR PRODUCTION USE** or biometric identification purposes.

## Prohibited Uses

**DO NOT USE THIS SYSTEM FOR:**

- **Biometric Identification**: Identifying or verifying individuals based on voice characteristics
- **Surveillance**: Monitoring, tracking, or surveillance of individuals
- **Voice Cloning**: Creating synthetic voices or impersonating individuals
- **Production Deployment**: Critical applications without proper validation
- **Unauthorized Audio Processing**: Processing audio without explicit consent
- **Discriminatory Practices**: Any form of discrimination based on audio characteristics

## Privacy Considerations

### Data Handling
- **No Persistent Storage**: Audio files are processed locally and not stored
- **No Personal Information**: No personally identifiable information is logged
- **Local Processing**: All computations happen on your local machine
- **No Network Transmission**: Audio data is not sent to external servers

### Research Ethics
- **Informed Consent**: Only process audio you have explicit permission to analyze
- **Transparent Use**: Clearly communicate the system's capabilities and limitations
- **Institutional Guidelines**: Follow your institution's guidelines for audio data research
- **Data Minimization**: Process only the minimum audio data necessary

## Technical Limitations

### Accuracy Limitations
- **Research Model**: Trained on limited datasets (ESC-50)
- **Generalization**: May not perform well on all audio types
- **Bias**: May exhibit bias towards certain audio characteristics
- **Validation**: Not validated for production use cases

### Security Considerations
- **No Authentication**: No user authentication or access controls
- **Local Access**: Anyone with access to the system can use it
- **Model Vulnerabilities**: May be susceptible to adversarial attacks
- **No Audit Trail**: Limited logging of system usage

## Responsible Use Guidelines

### Before Using This System
1. **Review This Disclaimer**: Understand the limitations and restrictions
2. **Check Permissions**: Ensure you have permission to process the audio
3. **Consider Alternatives**: Evaluate if this system is appropriate for your use case
4. **Document Usage**: Keep records of how and why you're using the system

### During Use
1. **Monitor Output**: Verify that results are reasonable and not biased
2. **Respect Privacy**: Do not process audio that could identify individuals
3. **Limit Scope**: Use only for the intended research or educational purpose
4. **Report Issues**: Document any problems or unexpected behavior

### After Use
1. **Delete Data**: Remove any audio files after processing
2. **Review Results**: Ensure results are used appropriately
3. **Document Findings**: Record any insights or limitations discovered
4. **Share Responsibly**: Only share results in appropriate contexts
